Our Story

Handcrafted in the shadows, finished for the light

Where craftsmanship meets artistry

Founded in Kanpur in 1995, Shadow Furniture emerged from a simple belief: furniture should outlive its owners. In a world of mass production, we chose the path less traveled — slow, deliberate, and deeply personal craftsmanship.

Every piece that leaves our workshop carries the signature of master artisans who have spent decades perfecting their craft. We work with solid hardwoods, natural finishes, and time-honored joinery techniques that have been passed down through generations.

What makes us different is what we leave out — excess ornamentation, trend-driven shapes, anything that distracts from honest form. Our work is quiet, but it stays with you.

Our guiding principles

Mission

To craft furniture that feels honest in the hand and quietly luxurious in the room — pieces that become heirlooms.

Vision

To become India's most respected bespoke furniture house — known for restraint, quality, and integrity.

Values

Integrity, patience, excellence, and a deep respect for the materials and people we work with.
