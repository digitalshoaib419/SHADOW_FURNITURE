FAQs

Common questions, answered

How long does a custom piece take?
Typical lead time is 6-8 weeks from design approval to delivery. Complex projects may take 10-12 weeks. We'll provide a clear timeline upfront.
Do you deliver pan-India?
Yes! We deliver across India with professional packaging and white-glove installation in major cities.
What materials do you use?
Only solid hardwoods — teak, sheesham, walnut, oak. No MDF or particle board. All finishes are premium and eco-friendly.
Can I see a sample before ordering?
Absolutely. We can send wood and fabric samples to your address or schedule a visit to our Lucknow workshop.
